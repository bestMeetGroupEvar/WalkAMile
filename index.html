Walk a mile

Excercise better, easier.

Walk a mile is a health related application assigning a user with a route of many stops and sports activities along the way.
After all, it's the same sports - just in a different way.

Our main mission is to change your usual sports, add variety and ultimately change the way you do sports.
Our goal is to get people motivated to be healthy and change their life

Become part of our fitness community, walk a mile and create your first step.
